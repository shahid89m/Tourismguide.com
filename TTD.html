THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT PAKISTAN

Things you should do Things you should avoid Pictures
May to October is generally considered the best
time to travel to Pakistan		 Do Not visit central Pakistan in August it is unbearable warm and humid
Carry your identification docments with you because visitors are often required to register at a number of check posts for your own safety. Dont get irritated by frequent questions asked by personnels in charge which can be irksome, just remember that all the precautions are for your own good.
Take note of cultural sensitivities. Don't travel by day during Ramadan.
Pakistan is safest place for tourist and solo female traveller. Due to security concerns,dont travel border close to Afghanistan.
Learn some common words of Urdu. Dont buy anything without bargaining.
During your trip to Pakistan, you’ll be invited for lunch, dinner and even to stay at people’s houses so many times that, on many occasions,let them be your host. Don't take advantage of their hospitality and interpret it correctly.
Try delicious and diverse Pakistani food full of spices espacially best chapli kebab found in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK) province. If you’re a strict vegetarian,tell your host beforehand.Otherwise, it will lead you to awkward situations when someone cooks up a meaty feast in your honor and you have to reject it.
In Pakistan,you should always have to be be respectful of Islam, the Prophet Muhammad, and religious cultures. Don’t ask for wine or bear openly because it is considered haram (prohibited by Islam) and not available everywhere.
